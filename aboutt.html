Rescue Dragons is a team of highly capable and motivated engineering youngsters.We are a group of 15 students in Velammal Engineering College.Our vision is to develop Unmanned Aerial Systems and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles for disaster relief and rescue operations.As they provide services in short time and can reach untracked places in a effective manner.In order to establish ourselves,our team is taking part in world level UAV drone competition,one such is UAV Medical Rescue 2020.We have been shortlisted among 47 well known teams worldwide for deliverable 1.And we are making progress towards further stages.
